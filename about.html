About Peace4Paws.

We’re an all-volunteer, foster-based dog rescue in Northern New Jersey, giving dogs in need the care, safety, and second chances they deserve since 2007.

Meet Our DogsSupport Our Work

Our mission

"Research Animals Make Exceptional Pets."

Founded in 2007, Peace4Paws is rooted in the yogic principle of ahimsa — nonviolence and compassion for all living beings. Our mission is to bring safety, healing, and peace to animals in need through rescue, foster care, adoption, education, and sanctuary support.

We began by helping animals retired from research and related fields, and that remains an important part of our mission. When laboratories are not releasing animals to us, we also help dogs from shelters, owner surrenders, and other difficult situations. Because our animals live in foster homes, the number of lives we can save depends on the availability of caring foster families.
