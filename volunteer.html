Why it matters

Every Hand Helps a Dog Get Home

Because we’re entirely volunteer-run and foster-based, our volunteers are the reason dogs get to vet visits, settle into foster homes, and find their families. Whether you can give an afternoon or a few hours a month, your time changes a life.

No experience needed for many roles — just a love of dogs and a willingness to help. We’ll show you the rest.
