Foster-based, all heart

Our dogs heal in homes, not kennels.

Because our dogs live with foster families, we get to know their personalities, routines, needs, and quirks. That helps us make thoughtful matches and give each dog the best chance at a lasting home.

Who we are

"Research Animals Make Exceptional Pets."

Founded in 2007, Peace4Paws began with a commitment to animals retired from research and related fields. Today, we continue that compassion through our foster-adoption program, helping shelter dogs, owner-surrendered dogs, and vulnerable animals receive care, safety, and second chances.
