Right now, more than 40 dogs are depending on Peace4Paws.

Peace4Paws runs entirely on donations, and your support helps us continue saying yes to dogs who need medical care, foster homes, and a second chance. Because we’re entirely volunteer-run, what you give goes directly to vet care, food, and rescue, never to salaries or offices.
